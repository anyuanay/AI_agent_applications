INCIDENT REPORT  --  case I-204  --  filed 2026-06-15T14:32:00Z

At 14:32 a hazardous-material spill (incident I-204) was reported on the
Main Street northbound segment. Incident Commander Diaz took command of
I-204 and designated evacuation zone EZ-7. Hazmat team HT-3 was dispatched
to I-204 and followed the hazardous-material protocol HMP-1. The fire
department FD-12 was also dispatched to I-204. Traffic camera CAM-90, which
monitors the Main Street northbound segment, recorded a reading of 47
vehicles per minute. The spill affects water main WM-7B. A bystander
reported what may be a second spill near 5th Avenue, but this is unconfirmed.
